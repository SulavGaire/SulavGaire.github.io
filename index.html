Electronic voting system using two factor biometric authentication-A case study in Nepal

[code] [report]

Major Project
Supervisor: Badri Raj Lamichhhane

  • Current System: Ballot paper-based voting in Nepal; insecure and time-consuming.
  • Proposed Solution: Web-based two-factor biometric authentication.
  • Biometric IDs: Fingerprint for voter info, face verification to cast vote.
  • Voter Registration: Records and stores fingerprint and face data using salted SHA256 hashing.
  • Evaluation: Tested with 50 participants; satisfaction surveys showed positive feedback.
  • Findings: Applications were intuitive, interactive, and improved voting security and integrity.
